Welcome to the Fishburners trial. You've become part of something special.

What's going on?

Bee Analytics is running a trial at Fishburners!

You're helping Fishburners better understand the facilities usage within our community. Think of it as lending a hand.

But tell me, how does it work?

Over the course of the trial, our Bee Stations will detect anonymous public wifi signals in the building. This data will be correlated with duration, time and location to build usage patterns of the Fishburner spaces and facilities.

Not to worry! We only use anonymised data and will not be able to identify or access any private information on your phone.

Though we guarantee anonymity and security, we understand this is not for everyone. You can opt-out of the trial at any time.
